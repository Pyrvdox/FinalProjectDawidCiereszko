TECH DESK

Rules for the use of office equipment


Purpose: This document outlines the rules and regulations for the proper and efficient functioning of the office environment.

Attendance and Punctuality: Employees are required to be punctual and maintain regular attendance. Notify your supervisor in advance for any planned absences or late arrivals.

Dress Code: Dress professionally and appropriately for your role. Follow the company's dress code policy, which may vary by department or job function.

Personal Belongings: Personal items should be kept tidy and organized. Avoid cluttering workspaces and common areas with excessive personal belongings.

Workstation Cleanliness: Keep your workstation clean and organized. Dispose of waste properly and store personal items discreetly.

Confidentiality: Respect the confidentiality of company, client, and employee information. Do not share sensitive information with unauthorized individuals.

Use of Common Areas: Common areas such as kitchens, break rooms, and meeting rooms should be left clean after use. Dispose of food waste properly.

Noise and Disturbances: Maintain a reasonable noise level to ensure a productive environment. Use headphones for personal audio and keep phone conversations discreet.

Meetings and Collaboration: Meetings should start and end on time. Be prepared, contribute constructively, and actively participate in discussions.

Internet and Network Usage: Use the office network for work-related activities. Avoid excessive personal internet usage that may affect productivity.

Smoking and Vaping: Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited within the office premises. Use designated smoking areas if available.

Visitor Policy: All visitors must sign in at the reception and be accompanied by an employee at all times. Notify the reception in advance for planned visits.

Health and Safety: Follow health and safety guidelines provided by the company. Report any unsafe conditions or incidents to your supervisor.

Eating and Drinking: Consume food and drinks in designated areas. Keep workstations and electronic devices free from spills and stains.

Personal Calls and Devices: Limit personal calls to designated break times. Ensure that personal devices do not disrupt your work or your colleagues'.

Office Equipment: Use office equipment responsibly and report any malfunctions to the IT department immediately. Do not remove office equipment from the premises without authorization.

Social Media and Online Activities: Exercise caution when engaging in social media activities related to the company. Do not share confidential or negative information online.

Grievance Procedure: If you have concerns or complaints, follow the company's established grievance procedure to address issues in a respectful and constructive manner.

Compliance: Failure to adhere to these rules and regulations may result in disciplinary action, up to and including warnings, suspension, or termination.



By working in this office, you acknowledge and agree to follow the rules and regulations outlined in this document.
